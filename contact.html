Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Can't find what you're looking for? Use our contact form above.

ü§ù Volunteer Questions

What's the time commitment for volunteering?

It varies by role. Mentors commit to 4-6 hours monthly for 6+ months. Skills-based volunteering can be project-based. Event support is flexible. We work with your availability.

Do I need specific qualifications to volunteer?

For mentorship roles, we prefer 2+ years of professional experience. For other roles, enthusiasm and commitment are most important. We provide training for all volunteers.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! We have remote opportunities in social media, content creation, administrative support, and virtual mentorship.

üéì Program Questions

Who is eligible for your programs?

South African youth aged 16-25, particularly from marginalized communities. We prioritize those with limited access to opportunities. No prior experience is required.

Are your programs free?

Yes, all our programs are completely free for participants. We're funded through donations, grants, and partnerships to ensure accessibility.

How do I apply for programs?

Complete our online application form, attend an information session, and participate in an interview. Visit our Programs page for details.

üíù Donation Questions

How are my donations used?

80% goes directly to program delivery, 12% to operational costs, and 8% to fundraising. We provide detailed financial reports to all donors.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, we're a registered NPO (123-456) and PBO (930123456). Donations are tax-deductible in South Africa. We provide receipts for all contributions.

Can I specify how my donation is used?

Absolutely! You can designate donations to specific programs (Digital Skills, Mentorship, Mental Wellness) or leave it unrestricted for greatest need.
