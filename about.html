Our Story: Building Hope Since 2021

RiseUp Youth Foundation emerged from a simple but powerful realization: every young South African deserves the opportunity to rise above their circumstances and build a future filled with promise and purpose.

From Vision to Impact: Our Journey

Founded in 2021 during the challenging aftermath of the COVID-19 pandemic, RiseUp Youth Foundation began as a grassroots initiative in Durban, South Africa. Our founders, educators, and community leaders witnessed firsthand how the pandemic exacerbated existing inequalities, particularly affecting youth from marginalized communities.

What started as weekend mentorship sessions for 15 young people has grown into a comprehensive youth empowerment organization reaching hundreds annually across KwaZulu-Natal. Our growth has been guided by listening to the needs of the communities we serve and adapting our programs to address the most pressing challenges facing South African youth today.

In 2022, we expanded our Digital Skills Academy in response to the growing digital divide. By 2023, we had established partnerships with local businesses and tech companies, creating pathways to employment for our graduates. Today, we operate multiple community centers and have launched satellite programs in three additional provinces.

Our Guiding Principles

Our Mission

To empower and uplift South African youth from marginalized communities by providing access to quality mentorship, education, digital skills training, and mental health support. We believe in creating sustainable pathways to success that transform not only individual lives but entire communities.

Our Vision

A South Africa where every young person, regardless of their background, has the resources, support, and self-assurance needed to overcome obstacles and create fulfilling, prosperous lives. We envision communities where youth are not just beneficiaries but active leaders in their own development.

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make and every program we develop:

Empowerment Through Education

We believe education is the most powerful tool for transformation. Our programs combine practical skills with critical thinking, preparing youth not just for jobs, but for leadership roles in their communities and beyond.

Inclusive Community

We actively reach youth from rural areas, townships, and underserved urban communities who face significant barriers to opportunity. Our spaces are designed to be safe, inclusive, and welcoming to all.

Meet Our Leadership Team

Our dedicated team brings together diverse expertise with a shared commitment to youth empowerment.
