Join Our Movement: Be Part of the Change

Every young person we empower represents a collective effort. Your involvement—whether through time, resources, or advocacy—creates ripples of positive change that transform communities across South Africa.

Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteers are the heartbeat of RiseUp. Your skills, passion, and time directly impact the lives of South African youth. We offer diverse volunteering roles to match different skills, schedules, and interests.

Become a Mentor

Time Commitment: 4-6 hours per month for 6 months minimum

Impact: Provide guidance, support, and real-world insights to youth navigating educational and career decisions.

Mentor Responsibilities:

  • Regular meetings with your mentee (in-person or virtual)
  • Career guidance and personal development support
  • Participation in group mentor activities
  • Progress tracking and feedback sessions

Ideal Candidates:

Professionals with 2+ years of work experience, educators, community leaders, or individuals with specialized skills to share.

Apply to Mentor

Skills-Based Volunteer

Time Commitment: Flexible - project-based or regular sessions

Impact: Share your professional expertise to enhance our programs and operations.

Skills Needed:

  • Tech Professionals: Coding instructors, IT support, web development
  • Educators: Tutoring, workshop facilitation, curriculum development
  • Creative Professionals: Graphic design, photography, content creation
  • Business Professionals: Marketing, finance, HR, strategy consulting

We match your specific skills with our most pressing needs, ensuring your contribution has maximum impact.

Share Your Skills

Event & Administrative Support

Time Commitment: Flexible hours, perfect for students or those with irregular schedules

Impact: Keep our operations running smoothly and help us reach more youth.

Opportunities Include:

  • Event planning and coordination
  • Office administration and data entry
  • Social media management and content creation
  • Fundraising campaign support
  • Community outreach and awareness building

No specific experience required—just enthusiasm and a willingness to help!

Support Through Donations

Your financial contributions directly fund programs, resources, and opportunities for South African youth. Every rand makes a difference in creating sustainable change.

One-Time Donation

Immediate Impact: Your single contribution provides immediate resources for our programs.

What Your Donation Provides:

  • R250: Learning materials for one youth for a month
  • R500: Internet data for digital skills training
  • R1,000: Mental health counseling sessions
  • R5,000: Equipment for our computer lab
Make a One-Time Donation

Monthly Sustainer

Sustainable Impact: Regular monthly donations provide predictable funding that allows for long-term planning.

Benefits of Monthly Giving:

  • Enables program expansion and sustainability
  • Reduces administrative costs
  • Provides stability for long-term youth support
  • You receive regular impact reports

Most Popular: R200/month supports one youth's entire program participation

Become a Monthly Sustainer

Corporate & Major Gifts

Transformative Impact: Larger contributions fund specific projects, infrastructure, or program expansion.

Opportunities for Major Support:

  • Matching gift programs

Your Donation at Work: Transparency and Impact

How We Use Your Contribution:

  • 80% Program Delivery: Direct services to youth
  • 12% Operational Costs: Staff, facilities, administration
  • 8% Fundraising: Growing our impact reach

We are committed to financial transparency and provide detailed annual reports to all donors. Every contribution, regardless of size, is acknowledged and stewarded with care.

Additional Ways to Make a Difference

Not ready to volunteer or donate? There are still many meaningful ways to support our mission.

Spread the Word

Awareness is a powerful form of support. Help us reach youth who need our programs and potential supporters who want to make a difference.

  • Follow and share our social media content
  • Tell friends and colleagues about our work
  • Invite us to speak at your organization or event
  • Share success stories within your networks

In-Kind Contributions

Non-monetary contributions can be equally valuable to our operations and programs.

  • Technology: Computers, tablets, software licenses
  • Educational Materials: Books, stationery, learning resources
  • Office Supplies: Furniture, equipment, consumables
  • Professional Services: Legal, accounting, marketing support

Contact us to discuss specific in-kind donation needs.

Legacy Giving

Ensure your support for South African youth continues for generations to come through planned giving.

  • Include RiseUp in your will or estate planning
  • Create a named endowment fund
  • Designate RiseUp as a beneficiary of retirement accounts or life insurance

These transformative gifts create lasting impact and can be structured to provide tax benefits.
